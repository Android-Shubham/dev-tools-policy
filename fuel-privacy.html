Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects user information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

The app may collect certain information to provide its core functionality.

  • Account information used to uniquely identify users
  • Fuel log data entered by the user (such as fuel amount, price, odometer readings, and dates)
  • Basic device information required for app functionality

Authentication

Authentication is required in order to uniquely identify users and securely store their data. This allows the app to associate fuel records and other information with the correct user account.

Authentication is handled through secure services and is used only for identifying users within the app.

Data Storage

User data is stored securely using Google Firebase. Firebase is a backend service provided by Google that helps manage and store application data.

Data stored in Firebase may include:

  • Fuel entries created by the user
  • User account identifiers
  • Application usage information necessary for functionality

Firebase services are governed by Google's privacy practices. You can learn more here:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Advertisements

This app may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These ads help support the continued development and maintenance of the app.

Advertising providers may collect anonymous data such as device identifiers and usage information to display relevant ads.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Store and synchronize user fuel data
  • Improve the app experience
  • Display advertisements

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the support section of the app.
